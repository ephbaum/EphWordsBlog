<p class="text-left italic leading-relaxed">
Is it suicide<br/>
for me<br/>
to enjoy cigarettes and weed<br/>
and alcohol in excess, perhaps too frequently?
</p>

<figure class="w-2/3 mx-auto opacity-80">

![Old Fashioned - Remedy](../../../../assets/images/2026/02/old_fashioned_remedy_md.jpg)

</figure>

<p class="text-right italic leading-relaxed">
I know the toll<br/>
yet I persist.<br/>
I'm only living my life.
</p>

<p class="text-left italic leading-relaxed">
I bargain with myself — I'll quit.<br/>
I've done it before;<br/>
I know the relief.
</p>

<p class="text-right italic leading-relaxed">
But vices give what I ask<br/>
then take more than I have,<br/>
until I let them go again —<br/>
if I live that long.
</p>

</div>
